Beer Science

The 20 Minute Rule

20 minutes is the optimal time to take a beer from room temperature to refreshing cold in a standard freezer without freezing it solid.

The Wet Towel Hack

Wrap a wet paper towel around the bottle. The evaporation cools it twice as fast. Set timer for 10-12 minutes if you do this!

Explosion Risk

Liquids expand when frozen. Leaving a glass bottle or aluminum can in too long can cause it to burst.
